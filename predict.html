💡Historical Survival Insights

Key factors that influenced survival on the Titanic

🎟️

Class Matters

1st class passengers had a 63% survival rate vs 24% in 3rd class. Proximity to lifeboats was decisive.

👩

Women First

"Women and children first" — female passengers had a 74% survival rate vs 19% for males.

🎂

Youth Advantage

Children under 10 had significantly higher survival rates. Age played a crucial role in boarding lifeboats.

💵

Fare & Location

Higher fares correlate with superior cabin locations and faster access to upper deck lifeboats.
